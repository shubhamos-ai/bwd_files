# ✨ Cosmic Astrology Analyzer

Welcome to the **Cosmic Astrology Analyzer**, a premium, glassmorphic web application designed to help you discover the hidden secrets of your birth date. This project blends ancient astrological wisdom with modern mathematical patterns, all within a stunning, responsive user interface.

## 🌟 Features

*   **Zodiac Calculation**: Instantly find your zodiac sign using a simplified, user-friendly calculation.
*   **Planet Analysis**: Get insights into your planetary alignment based on your celestial mapping.
*   **Death Calculator**: Discover your predicted lifespan through cosmic energy patterns.
*   **Premium Glassmorphism Design**: Experience a modern, "glassy" UI with deep blur effects, vibrant gradients, and smooth animations.
*   **Fully Responsive**: Works seamlessly on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.
*   **Simple Logic**: Built using clean, straightforward HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript logic (no advanced frameworks needed).

## 🌌 How to Use

1.  Open `index.html` in your favorite browser.
2.  Click **"Discover Your Sign"** to start the analysis.
3.  Choose your path (Zodiac, Planet, or Death Calculator).
4.  Enter your **name** and **birth date** in the glassy login-style form.
5.  Confirm the prompt to proceed and reveal your cosmic results!

## 🔭 Project Structure

*   `index.html`: The main landing page with a cosmic hero section and feature overview.
*   `form.html`: The multi-step container for selecting paths and entering details.
*   `result.html`: The final reveal screen for your celestial analysis results.
*   `about.html`: Information about the project's mission and team.
*   `style.css`: The "Cosmic" design system containing variables, keyframe animations, and glassmorphism styling.
*   `script.js`: The simple JavaScript engine that handles page logic, math, and data persistence.

## 🌠 Design Philosophy

The app is built with **Visual Excellence** in mind:
*   **Vibrant Palettes**: Deep indigos, magentas, and purples.
*   **Interactivity**: Hover effects and transitions that make the app feel "alive".
*   **Mobile-First**: Designed to be beautiful on every screen size.
