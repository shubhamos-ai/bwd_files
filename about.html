The Science Behind Zodiac Signs

Zodiac signs are based on the position of the sun at the time of your birth. The sun travels through 12 different constellations throughout the year, and whichever constellation it was in when you were born determines your zodiac sign.

Each zodiac sign spans approximately 30 days and has unique characteristics, strengths, and personality traits associated with it.
